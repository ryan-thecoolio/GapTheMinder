About APE Trading

The app is designed to educate and help people to critically think about the impact they have in common misconceptions about the world. For example, a study by Gapminder found that 86% of people believe most plastic waste is in the ocean. This untrue claim reflects society’s collective judgment of our future.

Thus, Ape Trading is a stock simulating app that combats people’s biases through an engaging simulation where individuals gain or lose money based on statements that are true or false respectively. We believe, the value of virtual currency can support user’s valuing the negative impacts of spreading misconceptions that prevent positive change for the future.
